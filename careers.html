Careers

Build healthcare infrastructure.
From day one.

QueueFree is an early-stage startup solving a real operational problem in Indian hospitals. We're looking for students who want to work on something that matters — not just add a line to their CV.

The Internship

Work at the infrastructure layer of healthcare.

As an intern at QueueFree, you work directly with the founding team on live product development, research, and operations. There are no layers between you and the actual problem inside Indian outpatient departments.

This is a hands-on role at a pre-revenue, pre-pilot stage startup — not a structured corporate programme. We're actively preparing our first hospital pilot in Tamil Nadu, and your work will contribute to that deployment.

Open Roles

Role 01

Healthcare Research Intern

Map OPD workflows, wait-time data, and hospital operational patterns across Tamil Nadu to ground QueueFree in real clinical environments.

Role 02

Product Intern

Work on product specs, user flows, and interface logic for both patient and hospital-side features — from token booking to queue dashboards.

Role 03

Operations Intern

Support pilot preparation, hospital outreach, and process documentation for the first deployment, working closely with hospital stakeholders.

Role 04

Software Development Intern

Contribute to MVP development across the patient app, hospital dashboard, and backend queue logic, with a focus on robustness over features.
