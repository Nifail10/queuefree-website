What We Build

Operational infrastructure for hospital outpatient departments.

Most hospitals manage patient flow through manual calling, paper tokens, or siloed software that offers no live visibility. QueueFree replaces that with a structured, real-time queue layer.

We are not a doctor marketplace. We are not telemedicine. We are infrastructure — the operational layer that runs underneath a hospital's outpatient workflow.

Core Capabilities

Pillar I

Live Queue State

Every token, every doctor, every department — visible in real time for both hospital staff and patients.

Pillar II

Delay Transparency

Doctors can mark delays. Patients receive immediate updates. Uncertainty is eliminated at the source.

Pillar III

Predictive Wait Estimation

Dynamic wait-time calculation based on live queue depth and historical consultation patterns.

Hospitals deserve the same operational clarity that modern logistics and service industries take for granted.
